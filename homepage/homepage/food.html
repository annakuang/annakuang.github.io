Famous Food in Chinatown

Chinatown is renowned for its delicious and authentic Chinese cuisine, offering a vibrant culinary experience that represents the diverse regional flavors of China. The neighborhood is home to some of the most iconic dishes that have been passed down through generations, making it a must-visit destination for food lovers.

Dim Sum

One of the most beloved offerings in Chinatown is dim sum, a collection of bite-sized dishes traditionally served in small steamer baskets or on small plates.

Roast Duck

Another must-try is roast duck, a dish that is a staple of Cantonese cuisine. The preparation of this dish is an art in itself.

Soup Dumplings

For those seeking something comforting and satisfying, soup dumplings (xiao long bao) are a must.
